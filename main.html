Paris,France


Paris,the City of Light,is one of the most iconic travel destinations in the world. It is known for its rich history,romantic ambiance ,stunning architecture,and world-class cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Effel Tower -the most famous landmark and a symbol of love.
  2. Louvre Museum -home to thousands of artworks including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral-a masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Elysees Avenue -a stunning street known for luxury shopping.
  5. Sein River Cruise-a romantic boat ride offering spectacular views.

Must -Try Foods

  • Croissants-buttery and flaky pastries enjoyed at breakfast.
  • Macarons-colorful and delicate french sweets.
  • Creme Brulee-a creamy desert with acaramelized sugar top.
  • Ratatouille-a delicious Provencal vegetabledish.
