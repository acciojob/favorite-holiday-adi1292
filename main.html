Maldives


The Maldives is a tropical paradise famous for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, luxurious resorts, and peaceful island life. It is the perfect getaway for relaxation, adventure, and unforgettable natural beauty.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Underwater Villas — unique ocean-view resorts built below sea level.
  2. Vaadhoo Island (Sea of Stars) — a magical beach glowing with bioluminescent waves.
  3. Banana Reef — one of the world’s best diving and snorkeling spots.
  4. Male Friday Mosque — a historic architectural masterpiece.
  5. Sunset Dolphin Cruise — an unforgettable experience watching playful dolphins.

Must-Try Foods

  • Mas Huni — a traditional breakfast of tuna and coconut.
  • Garudhiya — a fragrant tuna soup served with rice and lime.
  • Fihunu Mas — spicy grilled fish seasoned with Maldivian chili.
  • Hedhikaa — bite-sized Maldivian snacks enjoyed with tea.
  • Saagu Bondibai — a creamy dessert made from sago and coconut milk.
