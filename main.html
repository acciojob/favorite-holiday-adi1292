Goa


Goa is a beautiful coastal destination in India known for its beaches, nightlife, culture, and water sports.

  1. Baga Beach – famous for nightlife and water sports
  2. Fort Aguada – a historic 17th-century Portuguese fort
  3. Calangute Beach – ideal for relaxation and adventure
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – breathtaking natural waterfall
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus – UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Bebinca Dessert
  • Prawn Balchao
  • Chicken Xacuti
  • Vindaloo
